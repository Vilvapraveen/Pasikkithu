📖 Our Story

From Jaya's Kitchen
to Your Table

In 2020, during lockdown, Jaya realized her family's traditional recipes — passed down through four generations — were at risk of being forgotten. She began making small batches of Pirandai Thokku and Idli Podi to share with neighbors.

Word spread. What started as gifting turned into orders. Today, Jaya's Organic serves 500+ families with the same love, the same recipes, and the same promise: no shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromise.
